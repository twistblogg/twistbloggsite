### Section I : Add Contact Form Widget From Blogger Gadget Library



This section is for those who have not added contact form from blogger gadget library. I am assuming you are logged into your google accounts and have blogger.com opened in a tab.  


1) Go to **Layout** > Click on **Add A Gadget**.  
2) A popup will appear. Click on **More Gadgets** -> **Add** the widget.
